== Overview — Future Tense क्या है?

Future Tense का मतलब है — **आने वाले समय** की बात। जो काम होगा, हो रहा होगा, या तब तक हो चुका होगा — ये सब Future Tense में आता है।

Future Tense के 4 types हैं:

[cols="1,2,2"]
|===
| Type | कब Use करते हैं | Hindi Clue

| Simple Future
| आगे होगा / करेगा
| गा, गी, गे

| Future Continuous
| आगे हो रहा होगा
| रहा होगा, रही होगी, रहे होंगे

| Future Perfect
| तब तक हो चुका होगा
| चुका होगा, चुकी होगी, लिया होगा

| Future Perfect Continuous
| कब से हो रहा होगा
| रहा होगा + कब से / तक
|===

---

== 1. Simple Future Tense ✅

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: गा, गी, गे, ऊँगा, ऊँगी, एगा, एगी, एंगे

**English Signal Words**: tomorrow, next week, next month, next year, soon, later, tonight, in the future, I think, probably, I hope

NOTE: जब भी "कल करूँगा", "अगले हफ्ते होगा", "शायद" जैसी बात हो — Simple Future!

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: Future Prediction / Expectation
जो आगे होगा — अनुमान या उम्मीद।

* कल बारिश होगी। → _It will rain tomorrow._
* India ये match जीतेगा। → _India will win this match._

==== Situation 2: Instant / Spontaneous Decision
अभी decide किया — पहले से plan नहीं था।

* कोई door पर है। मैं खोलता हूँ। → _Someone is at the door. I will open it._
* तुम थके हुए हो? मैं चाय बनाता हूँ। → _You look tired. I will make you some tea._

==== Situation 3: Promise / Offer / Request
वादा, offer, या request।

* मैं promise करता हूँ, मैं late नहीं आऊँगा। → _I promise, I will not come late._
* क्या तुम मेरी मदद करोगे? → _Will you help me?_

==== Situation 4: Warning / Threat
चेतावनी देना।

* अगर तुमने पढ़ाई नहीं की तो fail हो जाओगे। → _If you don't study, you will fail._
* जल्दी करो, वरना bus छूट जाएगी। → _Hurry up, or you will miss the bus._

==== Situation 5: Facts about Future
Future में होने वाले facts।

* अगला World Cup 2027 में होगा। → _The next World Cup will be in 2027._
* मैं अगले साल 25 का हो जाऊँगा। → _I will turn 25 next year._

---

=== Will vs Going to — फ़र्क समझो 🤔

[cols="1,1"]
|===
| **Will** | **Going to**

| अभी decide किया (instant)
| पहले से plan है

| _I will have pizza._ (menu देखकर decide किया)
| _I am going to have pizza._ (पहले से सोच रखा था)

| Prediction (without evidence)
| Prediction (with evidence)

| _I think it will rain._ (लगता है)
| _Look at those clouds! It is going to rain._ (clouds दिख रहे — evidence)

| Promise / Offer
| Intention / Plan

| _I will help you._ (offer)
| _I am going to start gym from Monday._ (plan)
|===

TIP: Confusion हो तो simply **will** use करो — ज़्यादातर situations में दोनों acceptable हैं!

---

=== Pattern / Formula 📐

==== Affirmative ✅
----
Subject + will + V1 + Object
----
OR
----
Subject + is/am/are + going to + V1 + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं कल Delhi जाऊँगा।
| I will go to Delhi tomorrow.

| वो अगले month शादी करेगा।
| He will get married next month.

| हम Sunday को picnic पर जाएँगे।
| We will go for a picnic on Sunday.

| मैं तुम्हें बाद में call करूँगा।
| I will call you later.

| वो इस project पर काम करेगी।
| She will work on this project.
|===

==== Negative ❌
----
Subject + will + not + V1 + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं आज party में नहीं जाऊँगा।
| I will not (won't) go to the party today.

| वो अब और wait नहीं करेगा।
| He will not (won't) wait anymore.

| हम ये proposal accept नहीं करेंगे।
| We will not (won't) accept this proposal.

| बारिश शायद नहीं होगी।
| It probably will not (won't) rain.

| मैं तुमसे इस बारे में बात नहीं करूँगा।
| I will not (won't) talk to you about this.
|===

==== Interrogative ❓
----
Will + Subject + V1 + Object + ?
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| क्या तुम कल आओगे?
| Will you come tomorrow?

| क्या वो हमें help करेगा?
| Will he help us?

| Party कब होगी?
| When will the party be?

| तुम कहाँ रहोगे?
| Where will you stay?

| क्या वो exam pass करेगी?
| Will she pass the exam?
|===

---

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| I will goes tomorrow.
| I will *go* tomorrow.
| Will के बाद V1 (base form) — कोई -s/-es नहीं!

| He will went there.
| He will *go* there.
| Will के बाद V1, V2 नहीं!

| I will can do it.
| I will *be able to* do it.
| दो modals (will + can) एक साथ नहीं आते!

| She wills help you.
| She *will* help you.
| Will में कभी -s नहीं लगता!

| I am will go.
| I *will go*.
| am/is/are + will → wrong! सिर्फ will + V1
|===

---

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **Will = गा/गी/गे** — बस इतना formula काफ़ी है!
* 🎯 **Won't = Will not** — spoken English में won't ज़्यादा use होता है।
* 🎯 **"Shall" vs "Will":**
** Shall = formal / suggestions: _Shall we go?_ (चलें क्या?)
** Modern English में ज़्यादातर will ही use होता है। Shall सिर्फ I/We के साथ suggestions में: _Shall I help you?_
* 🎯 **First Conditional Pattern:** If + Simple Present, will + V1
** _If it rains, I will stay home._ (अगर बारिश हुई, तो मैं घर रहूँगा।)

== 2. Future Continuous Tense 🔄

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: रहा होगा, रही होगी, रहे होंगे

**English Signal Words**: at this time tomorrow, at 8 PM, by then, this time next week, when, while, still

NOTE: जब भी "कल इस time _हो रहा होगा_" जैसी बात हो — Future Continuous!

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: Future में किसी specific time पर काम चल रहा होगा
* कल इस time मैं flight में बैठा होऊँगा। → _This time tomorrow, I will be sitting in a flight._
* रात 10 बजे वो सो रही होगी। → _She will be sleeping at 10 PM._

==== Situation 2: Expected / Normal Action in Future
जो naturally होगा — plan नहीं, expectation है।

* मैं office जा रहा होऊँगा, तो रास्ते में तुम्हें pick कर लूँगा। → _I will be going to office, so I'll pick you up on the way._
* वो शाम को party attend कर रही होगी। → _She will be attending the party in the evening._

==== Situation 3: Polite Inquiry (Polite way of asking)
Politely पूछना कि कोई कुछ करेगा या नहीं।

* क्या तुम उसे ये message pass कर दोगे? → _Will you be passing this message to him?_
* क्या तुम कल office आ रहे हो? → _Will you be coming to the office tomorrow?_

==== Situation 4: Parallel Actions in Future
Future में दो काम एक साथ चल रहे होंगे।

* जब तुम exam दे रहे होगे, मैं तुम्हारा wait कर रहा होऊँगा। → _While you will be taking the exam, I will be waiting for you._

---

=== Pattern / Formula 📐

==== Affirmative ✅
----
Subject + will + be + V1+ing + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं कल इस time Delhi में घूम रहा होऊँगा।
| I will be roaming in Delhi at this time tomorrow.

| वो शाम 7 बजे खाना बना रही होगी।
| She will be cooking dinner at 7 PM.

| बच्चे स्कूल में पढ़ रहे होंगे।
| The children will be studying in school.

| हम अगले हफ्ते project पर काम कर रहे होंगे।
| We will be working on the project next week.

| वो अभी सो रहा होगा।
| He will be sleeping right now. _(guess about present!)_
|===

TIP: **अभी** के बारे में guess लगाना: _He **will be** sleeping right now._ — ये actually present के बारे में assumption है, future नहीं! ये special use है।

==== Negative ❌
----
Subject + will + not + be + V1+ing + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं कल काम नहीं कर रहा होऊँगा।
| I will not (won't) be working tomorrow.

| वो शाम को party attend नहीं कर रही होगी।
| She will not (won't) be attending the party in the evening.

| बच्चे उस time पढ़ नहीं रहे होंगे।
| The children will not (won't) be studying at that time.

| हम next week office नहीं आ रहे होंगे।
| We will not (won't) be coming to the office next week.

| वो अभी सो नहीं रहा होगा।
| He will not (won't) be sleeping right now.
|===

==== Interrogative ❓
----
Will + Subject + be + V1+ing + Object + ?
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| क्या तुम कल office आ रहे होगे?
| Will you be coming to the office tomorrow?

| क्या वो शाम को free होगी?
| Will she be free in the evening?

| तुम कितने बजे travel कर रहे होगे?
| What time will you be traveling?

| क्या बारिश हो रही होगी कल?
| Will it be raining tomorrow?

| कौन-कौन party attend कर रहे होंगे?
| Who all will be attending the party?
|===

---

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| I will being working.
| I will *be* working.
| will + be, not will + being

| She will be work tomorrow.
| She will be *working* tomorrow.
| be के बाद -ing ज़रूरी!

| Will he be go there?
| Will he be *going* there?
| -ing भूल गए

| I will be knowing by then.
| I will *know* by then.
| Know stative verb — continuous नहीं!

| He will be has a meeting.
| He will be *having* a meeting.
| has/have नहीं, having (V1+ing)
|===

---

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **Simple Future vs Future Continuous:**
** _I will eat dinner at 8._ → 8 बजे खाऊँगा (action)
** _I will be eating dinner at 8._ → 8 बजे खाना खा **रहा** होऊँगा (middle of action)
* 🎯 **Polite questions** बनाने का trick — Future Continuous ज़्यादा polite लगता है:
** _Will you **use** the car tonight?_ (direct)
** _Will you **be using** the car tonight?_ (polite — जैसे normally पूछ रहे हो)
* 🎯 **Formula एक ही है: will + be + V1ing** — सबके लिए same!

== 3. Future Perfect Tense ✨

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: चुका होगा, चुकी होगी, चुके होंगे, लिया होगा, दिया होगा, कर लिया होगा

**English Signal Words**: by tomorrow, by next week, by the time, by then, before, by 2027, by the end of

NOTE: जब बताना हो कि **future में किसी time तक काम complete हो चुका होगा** — Future Perfect!

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: Future में किसी time तक काम पूरा हो चुका होगा
* मैं शाम तक ये काम कर लूँगा। → _I will have finished this work by evening._
* वो अगले month तक 1000 books बेच चुकी होगी। → _She will have sold 1000 books by next month._

==== Situation 2: Before another future action
एक future action से **पहले** दूसरा complete हो चुका होगा।

* जब तुम आओगे, मैं खाना बना चुका होऊँगा। → _By the time you come, I will have cooked the food._
* जब वो call करेगा, हम पहुँच चुके होंगे। → _When he calls, we will have reached._

==== Situation 3: Achievement / Milestone by Future Date
Future में milestone बताना।

* 2027 तक मैं MBA कर चुका होऊँगा। → _By 2027, I will have completed my MBA._
* अगले साल तक हम 5 साल की शादी पूरी कर चुके होंगे। → _By next year, we will have completed 5 years of marriage._

==== Situation 4: Assumption about Past/Present (guess)
कोई काम अब तक हो गया होगा — guess लगा रहे हो।

* अब तक उसे mail मिल गया होगा। → _He will have received the email by now._
* वो अब तक पहुँच गई होगी। → _She will have reached by now._

---

=== Pattern / Formula 📐

==== Affirmative ✅
----
Subject + will + have + V3 + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं शाम 6 बजे तक report submit कर चुका होऊँगा।
| I will have submitted the report by 6 PM.

| वो अगले साल तक graduation कर चुकी होगी।
| She will have graduated by next year.

| जब तुम आओगे, मैं lunch कर चुका होऊँगा।
| By the time you arrive, I will have had lunch.

| हम December तक ये project complete कर चुके होंगे।
| We will have completed this project by December.

| अब तक उसे message मिल गया होगा।
| He will have received the message by now.
|===

==== Negative ❌
----
Subject + will + not + have + V3 + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं शाम तक ये काम पूरा नहीं कर पाऊँगा।
| I will not (won't) have finished this work by evening.

| वो तब तक नहीं पहुँची होगी।
| She will not (won't) have arrived by then.

| हम deadline तक project complete नहीं कर पाएँगे।
| We will not (won't) have completed the project by the deadline.

| बारिश शायद तब तक नहीं रुकी होगी।
| The rain will not (won't) have stopped by then.

| उसने अभी तक decide नहीं किया होगा।
| He will not (won't) have decided yet.
|===

----
Will + Subject + have + V3 + Object + ?
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| क्या तुम तब तक काम खत्म कर चुके होगे?
| Will you have finished the work by then?

| क्या वो अगले साल तक MBA कर चुका होगा?
| Will he have completed his MBA by next year?

| शाम तक कितने chapters complete हो चुके होंगे?
| How many chapters will have been completed by evening?

| क्या train तब तक आ चुकी होगी?
| Will the train have arrived by then?

| वो कब तक ये project finish कर चुकी होगी?
| By when will she have finished this project?
|===

---

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| I will have went.
| I will have *gone*.
| V3 (gone), V2 (went) नहीं!

| She will has finished.
| She will *have* finished.
| Will के बाद हमेशा have, has नहीं!

| By tomorrow I finished the work.
| By tomorrow I *will have finished* the work.
| Future है — will have + V3 चाहिए

| He will have complete the project.
| He will have *completed* the project.
| V3 (completed), V1 (complete) नहीं!

| I will have been finish by 5.
| I will have *finished* by 5.
| been + V1 → Future Perfect Continuous है, ये Future Perfect है!
|===

---

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **"By" = तक** — ये keyword दिखे तो Future Perfect सोचो!
** _**By** tomorrow, **By** next month, **By the time** you come..._
* 🎯 **Formula: will + have + V3** — सबके लिए same, कोई change नहीं!
* 🎯 **Future Perfect vs Simple Future:**
** _I will finish by 5._ → Simple Future (5 बजे खत्म करूँगा)
** _I will have finished by 5._ → Future Perfect (5 बजे तक खत्म हो **चुका** होगा)
** फ़र्क: "चुका" — completion emphasis!
* 🎯 **By now = अब तक** — Present situation guess करने के लिए: _She will have reached **by now**._

== 4. Future Perfect Continuous Tense ⏳

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: रहा होगा / रही होगी / रहे होंगे + **कब से / से / तक**

**English Signal Words**: for, since, by, by the time, by next year, how long, by then

NOTE: जब बताना हो कि **future में किसी point तक कोई काम कितने time से चल रहा होगा** — Future Perfect Continuous!

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: Future में किसी time तक duration बताना
Future point तक कितने time से काम continuous चल रहा होगा।

* अगले month तक मैं यहाँ 2 साल से काम कर रहा होऊँगा। → _By next month, I will have been working here for 2 years._
* December तक वो 6 months से training ले रही होगी। → _By December, she will have been training for 6 months._

==== Situation 2: Emphasizing Duration at a Future Point
Duration पर ज़ोर देना।

* 2027 तक हम 10 साल से एक-दूसरे को जान रहे होंगे। → _By 2027, we will have been knowing each other for 10 years._ ❌ (know stative है) → _By 2027, we will have known each other for 10 years._ ✅
* शाम 8 बजे तक मैं 4 घंटे से पढ़ रहा होऊँगा। → _By 8 PM, I will have been studying for 4 hours._

==== Situation 3: Cause-Effect in Future
Future में कोई result होगा, और उसकी वजह duration activity।

* आज शाम तक मैं थक जाऊँगा क्योंकि पूरा दिन काम कर रहा होऊँगा। → _I will be tired by this evening because I will have been working the whole day._

---

=== Pattern / Formula 📐

==== Affirmative ✅
----
Subject + will + have + been + V1+ing + for/since + Time
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| अगले March तक मैं यहाँ 3 साल से रह रहा होऊँगा।
| By next March, I will have been living here for 3 years.

| शाम 6 बजे तक वो 5 घंटे से पढ़ रही होगी।
| By 6 PM, she will have been studying for 5 hours.

| जब तुम आओगे, मैं 2 घंटे से wait कर रहा होऊँगा।
| By the time you come, I will have been waiting for 2 hours.

| 2030 तक वो 15 साल से teach कर रहा होगा।
| By 2030, he will have been teaching for 15 years.

| अगले हफ्ते तक हम 1 month से इस project पर काम कर रहे होंगे।
| By next week, we will have been working on this project for 1 month.
|===

==== Negative ❌
----
Subject + will + not + have + been + V1+ing + for/since + Time
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| तब तक वो ज़्यादा देर से काम नहीं कर रहा होगा।
| He will not (won't) have been working for too long by then.

| अगले month तक मैं ज़्यादा time से practice नहीं कर रहा होऊँगा।
| By next month, I will not (won't) have been practicing for much time.

| शाम तक बारिश ज़्यादा देर से नहीं हो रही होगी।
| It will not (won't) have been raining for too long by evening.

| December तक वो बहुत time से gym नहीं जा रही होगी।
| By December, she will not (won't) have been going to the gym for much time.

| हम बहुत देर से drive नहीं कर रहे होंगे तब तक।
| We will not (won't) have been driving for too long by then.
|===

----
Will + Subject + have + been + V1+ing + for/since + Time + ?

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| I will have been work for 2 hours.
| I will have been *working* for 2 hours.
| been के बाद V1+ing!

| He will have been working since 3 hours.
| He will have been working *for* 3 hours.
| Duration = for (3 hours, 5 years), Point = since (Monday, 2020)

| She will have been knowing him for years.
| She will have *known* him for years.
| Know stative verb — continuous में नहीं!

| They will has been waiting.
| They will *have* been waiting.
| Will के बाद have, has नहीं!

| By 2030, I will been working here for 10 years.
| By 2030, I will *have* been working here for 10 years.
| have भूल गए!
|===

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **Future Perfect vs Future Perfect Continuous:**
** _I **will have read** 5 books by December._ → **कितने** (count/result) → Future Perfect
** _I **will have been reading** for 3 months by December._ → **कितने time से** (duration) → Future Perfect Continuous
* 🎯 **Formula: will + have + been + V1ing** — सबके लिए exactly same!
* 🎯 **ये tense बहुत rarely use होता है** daily conversation में। लेकिन जानना ज़रूरी है — exams और formal writing में काम आता है!
* 🎯 **"By + future time" = तक / तब तक** — ये keyword दिखे, और duration भी हो, तो Future Perfect Continuous!
* 🎯 **Since vs For** — हमेशा same rule:
** **Since** = specific point (since 2020, since Monday, since morning)
** **For** = duration (for 2 hours, for 5 years, for ages)

== Final Summary — सब एक जगह 📋

[cols="2,2,2"]
|===
| Tense | Hindi Clue (Ending) | English Formula

| Simple Future
| गा, गी, गे
| will + V1

| Future Continuous
| रहा होगा, रही होगी
| will + be + V1+ing

| Future Perfect
| चुका होगा, लिया होगा
| will + have + V3

| Future Perfect Continuous
| रहा होगा + कब से
| will + have + been + V1+ing
|===

TIP: Future Tense सबसे आसान है — सब में **will** common है। बस will के बाद क्या लगा — V1, be+V1ing, have+V3, या have+been+V1ing — वो decide करता है tense!
