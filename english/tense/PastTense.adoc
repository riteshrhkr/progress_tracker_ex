== Overview — Past Tense क्या है?

Past Tense का मतलब है — **बीते हुए समय** की बात। जो काम हो चुका है, हो रहा था, या पहले हो चुका था — ये सब Past Tense में आता है।

Past Tense के 4 types हैं:

[cols="1,2,2"]
|===
| Type | कब Use करते हैं | Hindi Clue

| Simple Past
| काम हो गया (बीती बात)
| ता था, गया, किया, खाया, आया

| Past Continuous
| Past में कोई काम चल रहा था
| रहा था, रही थी, रहे थे

| Past Perfect
| एक काम दूसरे से पहले हो चुका था
| चुका था, चुकी थी, लिया था

| Past Perfect Continuous
| कब से हो रहा था (past में)
| रहा था + कब से / से
|===

---

== 1. Simple Past Tense ✅

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: गया, गई, गए, किया, खाया, बोला, आया, लिया, दिया, ता था, ती थी, ते थे

**English Signal Words**: yesterday, last week, last month, last year, ago, in 2020, in those days, when I was young, that day

NOTE: जब काम **बीत चुका है** और **कब हुआ** ये बताना है — Simple Past!

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: Completed Action in the Past
जो काम हो गया — खत्म।

* मैंने कल pizza खाया। → _I ate pizza yesterday._
* वो पिछले साल Japan गई। → _She went to Japan last year._

==== Situation 2: Past Habits / Routine
जो पहले regularly करते थे (अब नहीं)।

* मैं बचपन में रोज़ park जाता था। → _I went to the park daily in my childhood._
* वो पहले यहाँ काम करता था। → _He worked here before._

==== Situation 3: A Series of Past Actions (Story telling)
कहानी या sequence बताना — एक के बाद एक।

* मैं उठा, नहाया, और office चला गया। → _I woke up, took a bath, and went to office._
* उसने door खोला, अंदर आया, और बैठ गया। → _He opened the door, came in, and sat down._

==== Situation 4: Past Facts / Situations
जो past में सच था।

* मेरे दादाजी बहुत strong थे। → _My grandfather was very strong._
* उस time internet इतना fast नहीं था। → _The internet was not that fast at that time._

---

=== Pattern / Formula 📐

==== Affirmative ✅
----
Subject + V2 (Past form) + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैंने कल अपना room साफ किया।
| I cleaned my room yesterday.

| वो पिछले हफ्ते Delhi गया।
| He went to Delhi last week.

| हमने Sunday को party की।
| We had a party on Sunday.

| अंकिता ने exam बहुत अच्छा दिया।
| Ankita did very well in the exam.

| बच्चों ने पूरा दिन खेला।
| The children played the whole day.
|===

IMPORTANT: Simple Past में हमेशा **V2** (second form of verb) use करो। go → **went**, eat → **ate**, play → **played**

==== Negative ❌
----
Subject + did + not + V1 + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैंने कल खाना नहीं खाया।
| I did not (didn't) eat food yesterday.

| वो meeting में नहीं आया।
| He did not (didn't) come to the meeting.

| हमने वो movie नहीं देखी।
| We did not (didn't) watch that movie.

| बारिश नहीं हुई कल।
| It did not (didn't) rain yesterday.

| उसने मुझे call नहीं किया।
| She did not (didn't) call me.
|===

WARNING: Negative में **did** लगाने के बाद **V1** use करो, V2 नहीं! +
❌ I didn't went. → ✅ I didn't **go**.

==== Interrogative ❓
----
Did + Subject + V1 + Object + ?
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| क्या तुमने खाना खाया?
| Did you eat food?

| क्या वो कल आया था?
| Did he come yesterday?

| तुम कहाँ गए थे?
| Where did you go?

| उसने क्या कहा?
| What did she say?

| तुमने ये कब किया?
| When did you do this?
|===

---

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| I goed to school.
| I *went* to school.
| Go irregular verb है — V2 = went, goed नहीं!

| He didn't came.
| He didn't *come*.
| did के बाद V1 (come), V2 (came) नहीं!

| Did she went?
| Did she *go*?
| Same — Did के बाद V1!

| I was go there.
| I *went* there.
| Simple Past में is/am/are/was/were + verb नहीं लगता

| Yesterday I have eaten pizza.
| Yesterday I *ate* pizza.
| Yesterday → specific past time → Simple Past, not Present Perfect!
|===

---

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **"Did = V2 का काम कर देता है"** — जब did लगा दिया, verb V1 में रहेगा। Did ने past tense का काम ले लिया!
* 🎯 **Regular verbs** में V2 बनाना easy है — बस **-ed** लगाओ: play**ed**, watch**ed**, clean**ed**
* 🎯 **Irregular verbs** की V2 याद करने का कोई shortcut नहीं — रोज़ 10 verbs practice करो (V1, V2, V3)
* 🎯 **Was / Were** सिर्फ "to be" verb के लिए: _I **was** happy. They **were** tired._ — Action verbs के साथ was/were + V1 मत लगाओ!

== 2. Past Continuous Tense 🔄

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: रहा था, रही थी, रहे थे

**English Signal Words**: when, while, at that time, at 8 PM yesterday, all day yesterday, the whole evening

NOTE: जब past में कोई काम **चल रहा था** — बीच में था — तब Past Continuous!

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: Past में कोई काम चल रहा था
किसी specific time पर काम progress में था।

* मैं रात 10 बजे सो रहा था। → _I was sleeping at 10 PM._
* वो कल शाम को TV देख रही थी। → _She was watching TV yesterday evening._

==== Situation 2: दो काम एक साथ चल रहे थे (while)
दोनों काम parallel हो रहे थे।

* मैं खाना बना रहा था और वो TV देख रहा था। → _I was cooking and he was watching TV._
* जब बच्चे खेल रहे थे, माँ खाना बना रही थी। → _While the children were playing, mom was cooking._

==== Situation 3: Background action + Interruption (when)
एक काम चल रहा था, तभी दूसरा काम (sudden) हो गया।

* मैं नहा रहा था जब phone बजा। → _I was taking a bath when the phone rang._
* वो road cross कर रहा था जब accident हुआ। → _He was crossing the road when the accident happened._

==== Situation 4: Setting a Scene (Story में background)
कहानी में माहौल बताना।

* बारिश हो रही थी, हवा चल रही थी, और सब अंदर बैठे थे। → _It was raining, the wind was blowing, and everyone was sitting inside._

---

=== Pattern / Formula 📐

==== Affirmative ✅
----
Subject + was/were + V1+ing + Object
----

[cols="1,1"]
|===
| I/He/She/It → was
| You/We/They → were
|===

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं कल 9 बजे पढ़ रहा था।
| I was studying at 9 o'clock yesterday.

| वो office जा रहा था।
| He was going to the office.

| बच्चे बारिश में खेल रहे थे।
| The children were playing in the rain.

| माँ kitchen में चाय बना रही थी।
| Mom was making tea in the kitchen.

| हम उस time meeting कर रहे थे।
| We were having a meeting at that time.
|===

==== Negative ❌
----
Subject + was/were + not + V1+ing + Object
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| मैं उस time सो नहीं रहा था।
| I was not (wasn't) sleeping at that time.

| वो कुछ नहीं कर रही थी।
| She was not (wasn't) doing anything.

| बच्चे पढ़ नहीं रहे थे।
| The children were not (weren't) studying.

| बारिश नहीं हो रही थी।
| It was not (wasn't) raining.

| हम उसका wait नहीं कर रहे थे।
| We were not (weren't) waiting for him.
|===

==== Interrogative ❓
----
Was/Were + Subject + V1+ing + Object + ?
----

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Hindi | English

| क्या तुम सो रहे थे?
| Were you sleeping?

| वो क्या कर रही थी?
| What was she doing?

| क्या बारिश हो रही थी?
| Was it raining?

| तुम कहाँ जा रहे थे?
| Where were you going?

| कौन-कौन खेल रहे थे?
| Who all were playing?
|===

---

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| I were sleeping.
| I *was* sleeping.
| I के साथ was, were नहीं

| He was play cricket.
| He was *playing* cricket.
| -ing भूल गए

| When I was cooking, the phone was ringing.
| When I was cooking, the phone *rang*.
| Interruption (sudden action) Simple Past में — rang

| They was watching TV.
| They *were* watching TV.
| They के साथ were, was नहीं

| She was knowing the truth.
| She *knew* the truth.
| Know stative verb है!
|===

---

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **When vs While:**
** **While** → longer action (continuous): _**While** I was cooking..._
** **When** → shorter/sudden action (simple past): _...the phone rang **when**..._
** Pattern: **While + Past Continuous, Simple Past** या **Past Continuous + when + Simple Past**
* 🎯 **दो continuous actions?** दोनों में Past Continuous: _I was cooking **while** he was sleeping._
* 🎯 **Was/Were confusion?** बस याद रखो:
** **Was**: I, He, She, It (singular)
** **Were**: You, We, They (plural + you)

== 3. Past Perfect Tense 🔙

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: चुका था, चुकी थी, चुके थे, लिया था, दिया था, गया था, कर लिया था

**English Signal Words**: before, after, by the time, already, when, until, never...before, just

NOTE: जब **दो past actions** हों और बताना हो कि **कौन सा पहले** हुआ — Past Perfect (पहले वाले के लिए)!

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: पहले वाला काम (दो past actions में)
एक काम दूसरे से **पहले** हो चुका था।

* जब मैं station पहुँचा, train जा चुकी थी। → _When I reached the station, the train had already left._
* उसने खाना खा लिया था उससे पहले कि मैं पहुँचता। → _He had eaten food before I arrived._

==== Situation 2: Experience till that point in past
किसी past point तक का experience।

* 2020 तक मैंने कभी flight नहीं ली थी। → _I had never taken a flight until 2020._
* उस time तक वो 3 companies में काम कर चुका था। → _By that time, he had already worked in 3 companies._

==== Situation 3: Reported Speech / Indirect Speech
कोई बात past में किसी ने कही थी।

* उसने बताया कि वो exam pass कर चुका था। → _He told me that he had passed the exam._
* Teacher ने कहा कि उसने homework नहीं किया था। → _The teacher said that he had not done his homework._

==== Situation 4: Third Conditional (काश...)
जो हो नहीं पाया — regret बताना।

* काश मैंने harder पढ़ाई की होती। → _If I had studied harder, I would have passed._
* काश वो time पर आ गया होता। → _If he had come on time, we would have caught the train._

|===

---

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| When I reached, the train has left.
| When I reached, the train *had* left.
| Past की बात है → had, not has

| I had went there before.
| I had *gone* there before.
| had के बाद V3 (gone), V2 (went) नहीं!

| He had ate lunch.
| He had *eaten* lunch.
| Eat का V3 = eaten

| Before I arrived, he left.
| Before I arrived, he *had* left.
| पहले वाले action को had + V3 से mark करो

| I had been to Goa yesterday.
| I *went* to Goa yesterday.
| Simple one-time past action with time → Simple Past enough है
|===

---

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **Past Perfect = "Past का Past"** — जो Simple Past से भी **पहले** हुआ!
* 🎯 **Formula याद करो: had + V3** — सबके लिए same (I had, he had, they had — कोई change नहीं!)
* 🎯 **Before/After trick:**
** _He **had eaten** before I came._ (had eaten → पहले वाला action)
** _After he **had eaten**, he went to sleep._ (same — after + had V3)
* 🎯 **अगर दोनों actions sequence में clear हैं, Simple Past भी चलता है:** _I came home and ate dinner._ — ये भी correct है। Past Perfect ज़रूरी नहीं हमेशा, बस जब emphasis करना हो कि **पहले** हुआ।

== 4. Past Perfect Continuous Tense ⏳

=== पहचान कैसे करें? 🔍

**Hindi में ending**: रहा था / रही थी / रहे थे + **कब से / से / पिछले...**

**English Signal Words**: for, since, before, when, how long, all day, the whole morning

NOTE: ये तब use करो जब बताना हो कि past में किसी event से **पहले कितने time** से कोई काम चल रहा था।

=== कब Use करें? 📌

==== Situation 1: Duration before a past event
एक past event से पहले कोई काम कितने time से चल रहा था।

* जब वो आया, मैं 2 घंटे से wait कर रहा था। → _When he arrived, I had been waiting for 2 hours._
* उसने job छोड़ दी जहाँ वो 5 साल से काम कर रहा था। → _He quit the job where he had been working for 5 years._

==== Situation 2: Cause of Past Result (किसी past result की वजह)
Past में कोई result था, और उसकी वजह ये थी कि काम कुछ देर से चल रहा था।

* वो थका हुआ था क्योंकि वो 3 घंटे से दौड़ रहा था। → _He was tired because he had been running for 3 hours._
* उसकी आँखें लाल थीं क्योंकि वो रो रही थी। → _Her eyes were red because she had been crying._

==== Situation 3: Emphasis on Duration (ज़ोर देना कि कितने time तक)
Duration emphasis करना past context में।

* वो 10 साल से एक ही company में काम कर रहा था जब transfer हुआ। → _He had been working in the same company for 10 years when he got transferred._
* मैं सुबह से खाना बना रहा था जब guests आए। → _I had been cooking since morning when the guests arrived._

=== Common Mistakes ⚠️

[cols="1,1,2"]
|===
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right | Problem क्या है

| I had been wait for 2 hours.
| I had been *waiting* for 2 hours.
| been के बाद V1+ing!

| He had been working since 3 hours.
| He had been working *for* 3 hours.
| Duration = for, Time point = since

| She had been knew him for years.
| She *had known* him for years.
| Know stative verb — continuous नहीं!

| They had being working all day.
| They had *been* working all day.
| being नहीं, been!

| I had been studied since morning.
| I had been *studying* since morning.
| V3 नहीं, V1+ing!
|===

=== Tips & Tricks 💡

* 🎯 **Past Perfect Continuous = "Past में कब से"** — ये याद रखो बस!
* 🎯 **Past Perfect vs Past Perfect Continuous — फ़र्क:**
** _I **had read** 3 books before the exam._ → **कितने** (count/result) → Past Perfect
** _I **had been reading** for 3 hours before the exam._ → **कितने time से** (duration) → Past Perfect Continuous
* 🎯 **Since vs For** — same rule as Present Perfect Continuous:
** **Since** = point of time (since morning, since 2020)
** **For** = duration (for 2 hours, for 5 years)
* 🎯 **ज़्यादातर sentences में "when" या "before" से दूसरा action जुड़ता है:** _I had been waiting for 2 hours **when** he finally came._

=== Questions

1. मैंने कल अपना phone घर पर भूल दिया।
2. वो शाम 6 बजे खाना बना रही थी।
3. जब मैं पहुँचा, meeting खत्म हो चुकी थी।
4. वो 3 घंटे से सो रहा था जब alarm बजा।
5. बच्चों ने पूरा दिन cricket खेला।
6. क्या तुम उस time काम कर रहे थे?
7. उसने बताया कि वो Delhi जा चुका था।
8. मैं बारिश में भीग रहा था।
9. जब lights गई, हम 1 घंटे से movie देख रहे थे।
10. क्या उसने कभी Goa visit नहीं किया था?
11. मैं खाना खा रहा था जब doorbell बजी।
12. वो 2019 से इस company में काम कर रहा था जब उसे promotion मिला।

=== Answers

[%collapsible]
====
1. I forgot my phone at home yesterday. _(Simple Past — completed past action)_
2. She was cooking food at 6 PM in the evening. _(Past Continuous — specific time पर चल रहा था)_
3. When I reached, the meeting had already finished. _(Past Perfect — पहले हो चुका था)_
4. He had been sleeping for 3 hours when the alarm rang. _(Past Perfect Continuous — कब से + when)_
5. The children played cricket the whole day. _(Simple Past — completed action)_
6. Were you working at that time? _(Past Continuous — Interrogative)_
7. He told me that he had been to Delhi. _(Past Perfect — reported speech)_
8. I was getting wet in the rain. _(Past Continuous — चल रहा था)_
9. When the lights went off, we had been watching a movie for 1 hour. _(Past Perfect Continuous — duration before past event)_
10. Had he never visited Goa before? _(Past Perfect — Interrogative + never)_
11. I was eating food when the doorbell rang. _(Past Continuous + when + Simple Past — interruption)_
12. He had been working in this company since 2019 when he got a promotion. _(Past Perfect Continuous — since + when)_
